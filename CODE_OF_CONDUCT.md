# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want depfresh to be a welcoming, friendly, and productive project for
everyone who takes part — contributors, maintainers, and users alike. We pledge
to make participation a positive experience regardless of background or
experience level.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community thrive:

- Being kind, patient, and respectful toward others.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Assuming good intent and focusing on what's best for the project.
- Showing empathy and courtesy in all interactions.

Behavior that is not acceptable includes:

- Personal attacks, insults, or demeaning comments.
- Harassment of any kind, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests,
discussions, and code review — and whenever someone is representing the project
in public.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for upholding these standards and may edit, remove,
or reject contributions and comments that don't align with them. In serious or
repeated cases, a contributor may be temporarily or permanently barred from
participating.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the
maintainer at **hemachandar.pamanji@gmail.com**. All reports will be handled
discreetly and reviewed in good faith.
